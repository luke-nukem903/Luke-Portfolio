About Me

Born in Texas in 1994, grew up in the Dallas/Fort Worth area, with my sister and parents. Graduated Van Alstyne High School in 2012, decided school was not for me, and began life as an Automotive Technician, worked at a few different "Mom-and-Pop" shops. At that time I decided that was the career for me (obviously not here I am). Started working with Mazda as a technician in 2018 and advanced to the highest level they offer: Master Certified. Working in a dying industry (look up nation-wide technician shortage) taught me that it is never too late to change career paths as long as you are willing to put the work in so, I decided to go back to school and try my hands at coding.
